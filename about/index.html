WE ARE GAMERS

Frontline Gaming is a dedicated E-sports team looking to give amateur players a place to shine and thrive. Hopefully with enough recognition, we can give these players opportunities to grow, where they can not get anywhere else.

TOURNAMENTS

This paragraph explains how the company throws online tournaments where players can win money and get recognized by other professional E-sports teams. Tournaments have been rising in the community and many players are able to make enough to live off of these winnings

CONTENT CREATORS
